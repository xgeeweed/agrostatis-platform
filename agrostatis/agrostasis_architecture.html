Figure 1 (v0.4). AGROSTASIS system architecture. The Modeling Layer now correctly contains three inference components: the Spatio-Temporal GP Engine, Multi-Modal Data Fusion, and the Biology Models (Z₁–Z₄) — all of which produce probabilistic posteriors over soil state, not actions. The Decision Layer contains only the two action-producing agents: Sampling (where to observe) and Fertility (what to apply). The Orchestrator Agent sits between Modeling and Decision, holding beliefs about field management state and running its heartbeat cycle to ensure nothing goes unobserved, unactioned, or unreconciled. Its free energy rises when zones go stale, interventions go unfollowed, or agent beliefs diverge — and it acts to correct this without waiting to be asked.
